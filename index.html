About Me

Dedicated Public Servant

Dr. Sukhato A. Sema is a respected leader, dedicated public servant, and academician who serves as the MLA from the Zünheboto-III A/C. With a background in academia, he transitioned into public service with a mission to bring transformative change and sustainable development to his constituency and Nagaland at large.

His approach to governance is rooted in inclusivity, transparency, and a deep understanding of the region's unique cultural heritage and developmental needs. Dr. Sema believes in the power of education and innovation as catalysts for societal progress.

As an MLA, he has consistently advocated for policies that promote equitable growth, environmental sustainability, and the preservation of indigenous knowledge systems while embracing modern development paradigms.

Vision & Initiatives

Education

Transforming educational infrastructure and improving access to quality education at all levels, with special focus on skill development and vocational training.

Healthcare

Enhancing healthcare facilities and services in rural areas, promoting preventive healthcare, and ensuring affordable medical care for all citizens.

Youth Empowerment

Creating opportunities for youth through entrepreneurship programs, sports initiatives, and cultural exchanges to harness their potential as future leaders.

Infrastructure

Developing sustainable infrastructure that connects remote areas, improves living standards, and creates a foundation for economic growth.
